Final Design

this is my final design. I decided to use the first one i created as I really liked the simplicity of it. After receiving feeback about areas I could improve I changed a number of things. Firstly, I had not put anything behind the text on the left hand side of the page, meaning it was transparent, I changed this to white. The logo looked cluttered with the balloons covering the text so I took these out in photoshop and placed them elsewhere, giving the poster a cleaner look. I made the sponsor logos smaller to give emphasis to the main content. Lastly, I moved the text from the bottom and aligned it with the dates, also making them all a lot bigger.
